I, Geraldine Callegari am a professional graphic designer. I am passionate about design and visual expression is an important part of my life. I have skills, passion, determination and a love for design. I specially enjoy illustration, image manipulation and photography. Besides graphic design, my other interests are drawing , painting and craft.

 

Many of my skills developed through my experiences interning with Goddess magazine and working in

Pen of America company. To me everyday and every circumstance is an opportunity to learn. I believe that  motivation is the most powerful tool that a person can have. A motivated person can go through all his limitations and fears. How motivated a person can be determine how successful a person will be.
